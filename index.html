ISEE Optimizer 2026

Calculate your ISEE, Child Allowance (AUU) and Nursery Bonus, and optimize with the new BTP exclusion.

About

ISEE Optimizer 2026 helps Italian households calculate their ISEE and estimate welfare benefits such as the Assegno Unico Universale and the Bonus Nido. It also shows how the new exclusion of government bonds (BTP) up to 50,000 euro, introduced by the 2026 Budget Law, can affect your ISEE and benefits. All calculations run on your device.
